Recipe #1: Chocolate Cake

  • Time: 1 hour
  • Serves: 8
Ingredients Quantity
All-purpose flour 3 cups
Granulated sugar 3 cups
Unsweetened cocoa powder 1 1/2 cups
Baking soda 1 tablespoon
Baking powder 1 1/2 teaspoons
Salt 1 1/2 teaspoons
Large eggs 4
Buttermilk 1 1/2 cups
Warm water (or brewed coffee) 1 1/2 cups
Vegetable oil 1/2 cup
Vanilla extract 2 teaspoons

Step 1: Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).

Step 2: Mix all the dry ingredients together.

Step 3: Add wet ingredients and mix until smooth.

Step 4: Pour batter into prepared pans and bake for 30-35 minutes.

Recipe #2: Pasta Primavera

  • Time: 30 minutes
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Carrots, peeled and cut into thin strips 3
Medium zucchini (or 1 large zucchini), cut into thin strips 2
Yellow squash, cut into thin strips 2
Onion, thinly sliced 1
Yellow bell pepper, cut into thin strips 1
Red bell pepper, cut into thin strips 1
Olive oil 1/4 cup
Kosher salt and freshly ground black pepper To taste
Dried Italian herbs or herbes de Provence 1 tablespoon
Farfalle (bowtie pasta) 1 pound
Cherry tomatoes, halved 15
Grated Parmesan 1/2 cup

Step 1: Boil the farfalle pasta in salted water until al dente.

Step 2: In a large skillet, heat olive oil and sauté the vegetables until tender.

Step 3: Add cooked pasta, cherry tomatoes, and herbs. Toss to combine.

Step 4: Serve with grated Parmesan on top.
